I'm a **VLSI Design & Verification Engineer** with hands-on experience in **pre-silicon verification**, **RTL design**, and **AI-driven EDA tool development**. I independently developed **AIDEA** — an AI-assisted platform integrating Yosys, PyVerilog, and local LLMs for RTL analysis, logic visualization, and FPGA/ASIC estimation. My UVM/SystemVerilog testbenches have achieved **96% functional coverage**, isolating **4 pre-synthesis RTL bugs** across CNN, k-NN, and Decision Tree inference accelerator modules.

With **4 peer-reviewed publications** on **Elsevier**, **IEEE Xplore (×2)**, and **Springer**, a government-funded research grant (₹8 Lakhs, MSME), and hands-on FPGA deployment on Xilinx ZYNQ-7000, I bring depth across the full RTL-to-silicon workflow.

### ⚡ FPGA-Based ML Inference Accelerator — Binary Image Classification

> *Neural Networks deployed at the edge — on silicon*

End-to-end ML inference pipeline on **Xilinx ZYNQ-7000** with a custom Binary Neural Network (BNN) architecture. Built a full **UVM verification environment** (agent, driver, monitor, scoreboard, coverage collector) for 3 RTL compute modules (CNN, k-NN, Decision Tree). ### ⚡ Design & Optimization of Low-Power Adder Architectures

> *Squeezing every milliwatt out of silicon*

Designed and synthesised **mux-based ripple carry adder** architectures in Verilog; benchmarked PPA metrics using **Cadence Genus** across multiple design variants — evaluating critical path delay and power-area trade-offs. Applied **clock gating** and **operand isolation** to reduce switching activity, achieving an **~18% reduction in dynamic switching power** over the baseline design while maintaining timing closure at the target frequency.

**Tech Stack:**  
![Verilog](https://img.shields.io/badge/-Verilog-FF6B35?style=flat-square) ![Cadence Genus](https://img.shields.io/badge/-Cadence%20Genus-FF6F00?style=flat-square) ![CMOS Design](https://img.shields.io/badge/-CMOS%20Design-37474F?style=flat-square)

**🎯 Impact:** 18% dynamic power reduction · PPA benchmarking across adder variants

</td>

### 🍋 Fruit Ripeness Classification using Deep CNNs

> *AI-powered precision agriculture at the edge*

Comparative study of **EfficientNetB0** vs. **ResNet50** transfer learning models for multi-class fruit ripeness classification. Top model achieved **78.67% test accuracy** — a **13.35% improvement** over baseline on the same dataset. Deployed via a **Flask web application** for real-time inference in agricultural automation pipelines.
